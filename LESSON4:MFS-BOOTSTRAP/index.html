My Journey

For more than 40 years, My First Skool has been a leader in early childhood care and education. As the pre-school arm of NTUC (National Trades Union Congress) First Campus Co-operative, we have grown into one of Singapore's most respected early childhood organisations since we opened our doors in 1977.

>
Over 140
Pre-schools Islandwide

Dedicated to bringing quality pre-school education within easy reach, we have over 140 pre-schools islandwide, and more every day.

>
Pre-schools with a
difference

To enable all-round development in our children, our carefully devised curriculum allow them to learn through non-traditional modes and further develop their areas of interests, be it in STEM, arts, nature, bilingualism or cultural intelligence.

>
Serving more than
20,000 families

We are proud to bring quality education to more than 20,000 young families in every Singapore neighbourhood.
